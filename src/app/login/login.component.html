Study Title: Advancing Collective Innovation

 

Principal Investigator: Dr. Steven Dow

_______________________________________________________________________________

 

Purpose of this Study

The purpose of this study is to evaluate tools and procedures doing collaborative brainstorming online. Please read this form and ask any questions that you have before agreeing to be in the study. The study is being conducted by Prof. Steven Dow from UC San Diego.

 

Procedures

Participants will perform individual and collaborative brainstorming tasks. This will involve reading information and viewing ideas related to an important social problem. You will work with a partner to improve your ideas. The work will take approximately 15 minutes. A few surveys will also collect a few demographic details. All participation is optional. You may drop out at any time. Participants will only receive compensation if they complete the task.

 

Participant Requirements

To participate in this study, we ask that you satisfy the following criteria:

         You must be at least 18 years of age.

         You must be willing and able to use a standard computer.

 

Risks

The risks and discomfort associated with participation in this study are no greater than those ordinarily encountered in daily life or during other online activities. You may discontinue the study at any time.

 

Benefits

There may be no personal benefit from your participation in the study. The knowledge generated in this research may be of value to humanity.

 

Compensation & Costs

Participants will be compensated 10 cents for waiting for a partner and $2 for the entire task.

 

Confidentiality

The physical and electronic records of this study will be kept private. All the collected designs and critiques will be anonymized in any publication. Subjects names are not recorded with their responses. In any report that we might publish, we will not include any information that will make it possible to identify a subject. Records will be kept in a locked file in a locked office or on a secure computer system; only researchers named on this form will have access to the records.
